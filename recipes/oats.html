Powerful Protein Overnight Oats

Description

Prepare your taste buds for this protein packed adult cereal you'll surely enjoy every morning! With at least 20G of protein per each serving, you'll be eating a very nutritious breakfast when combining whey protein with oats. This is a true muscle building meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of old fashioned oats
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 2 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 35ml of sugar free maple syrup
  • 1 cup of milk of your choice

Steps

  1. Input oats, protein powder, peanut butter, and maple syrup into a mason jar.
  2. Pour one-half cup of the milk into the mason jar, put on the lid, and shake until mixture is evenly incorporated.
  3. Pour the remaining half cup of milk into the jar, and shake until mixture is evenly incorporated.
  4. Store in the fridge for at least 8 hrs overnight, and enjoy the next morning!
