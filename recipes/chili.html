Branden's Special Chili Recipe

Description

Making a big pot of chili that will soothe the soul is not as hard as one would think. All you need is a few of the usual ingredients necessary for making chili, less than one minute of prep time, and ten minutes of cooking time. I present to you, the best damn lazy man chili receipe you'll find!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground meat of your choice
  • 1 can of pinto beans
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 24 oz jar of salsa
  • 1 packet of chili seasoning
  • a few handfuls of frozen onions and peppers
  • 1 pouch of 90 second rice of your choice

Steps

  1. To prep, drain beans. Yup, that's it.
  2. Add your choice of cooking oil to the pot and heat it at medium-high heat. Add the frozen onions and peppers and cook until they turn translucent, which should only take a few minutes.
  3. Add the ground meat and seasoning it to taste
  4. Once your meat is cooked, add the following ingredients:
    • chili seasoning
    • salsa
    • fill half of the salsa jar with water
  5. Stir the pot and put a lid on it. Cook for 5 mins.
  6. Turn the heat down to low-medium heat. Stir the pot and put the lid back on. Cook for another 5 mins.
  7. Let the pot cool down for a few mins, and you're ready to eat! Add your favorite chili toppings like cheese, crackers, hot sauce, raw onions, etc.
