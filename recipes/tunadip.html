Ranch Tuna Dip

Description

This dip is sure to be a crowd pleaser at your next get together, but can also be enjoyed as an everyday snack! You can try this recipe with other types of canned meat (like chicken) in place of tuna, but I've found tuna to be the best choice for this dip.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of solid albacore tuna in water
  • 2 tbsp of flaxseed
  • 1 6 oz container of plain fat free greek yogurt
  • 1/3 packet of ranch seasoning

Steps

  1. Drain tuna and add it to a bowl.
  2. Add remaining ingredients, and mix until evenly incorporated.
  3. Enjoy with tortilla chips, crackers, in a tortilla wrap, with veggies, etc.
